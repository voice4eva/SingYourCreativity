"It has been a great joy to see my students discover their talents, often deeply hidden or restrained." ~ Eva Klirowska
Eva is a professional singer, choir conductor and teacher. She has 20 years of experience in teaching music to adults and adolescents. Over her career she has performed in the USA, Switzerland Poland. She practices singing with passion and uses it as therapy.
