Every Monday evening in Monthey!

Singing together for your well-being

Explore your musical sensitivity through song and body awareness. These are wonderful tools for connecting to the deep parts of your body and soul! Come to meet us on any Monday evening in the Torrent music room on the site of Malevoz in Monthey. We will be happy to welcome you to sing, dance, laugh and meditate with us!

New registrations are starting now, so if you've always dreamed of working on your voice, now you have the opportunity to get started in a friendly atmosphere and under the watchful eye of a competent vocal teacher!
