What is it?

Sing Your Creativity is an interactive, music-based workshop that promotes teamwork and creativity in a fun atmosphere. SYC gives you a break from the daily routine, and allows you to interact with your peers in a creative setting. Anyone and everyone is welcome to try. And singing or dancing skills aren't required!

Why do this?

Substitute creativity for burnout

When we sing, we feel inspired and motivated. And this only grows stronger when we sing in harmony with other people as positive energy is contagious. Sharing this experience with your colleagues revitalizes your state of mind and allows creative thoughts in!

Take teamwork to new heights

Music is known to relieve stress. This workshop provides a fun, carefree environment while engaging in an interactive teamwork-oriented project. This is a perfect way to exchange ideas and work with your colleagues!

Improve your health

Giving your body a workout through singing improves your concentration abilities and takes away many physical and psychological tensions. Singing and dancing stimulates our brains to increase the production of endorphins and serotonins, nature's “happy hormones” and pain killers.
