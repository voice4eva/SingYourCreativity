SYC Regenerating Weekends

Spend your weekend in the mountains! Our Regenerating Weekends feature a whole host of activites designed around our music workshop that are not only meant to stimulate your creativity, but also give you some well-deserved rest and recreation.

Content of the workshop:

(10 people minimum, 25 people maximum), CHF 170 participation fee.
Saturday:
  •  9:00: Arrival and welcome with coffee and croissants.
  • 10:00: Short hike in the area with pauses for breathing and stretching exercises.
  • 12:00: Homemade lunch back at the chalet. (We have awesome cooks!)
  • 13:30: Break. (We'll have your beds made in case you're in the mood for a siesta!)
  • 14:30: SYC workshop part 1.
  • 16:00: Coffee break.
  • 16:30: SYC workshop part 2.
  • 18:00: Free time.
  • 19:00: Supper. Followed by musical animation and singing.
Sunday:
  •  8:00: Breakfast.
  •  9:00: Stretching outside. (Don't forget your yoga mat...)
  • 10:00: Coffee break.
  • 10:30: SYC workshop part 3.
  • 12:00: Lunch.
  • 13:30: Break. (Siestas are encouraged!)
  • 14:30: SYC workshop part 4.
  • 16:00: End our weekend with coffee, tea and pastries.

Registration and payment:

Your registration will be finalized upon receipt of payment of 170 francs, covering all the activities and full-board accommodation during the weekend.

(Further information will be communicated with you upon your registration.)
